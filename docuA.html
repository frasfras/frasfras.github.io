How images are processed and divided into slices

It loops through each slice of the image. For each slice:
It adjusts the vertical position (y) based on a sine wave, creating a curved effect.
It alternates the skew direction for odd and even slices, creating a zigzag folding effect.
It adjusts the horizontal position (x) based on the skew angle, which helps maintain the image's overall width.
It applies a color filter based on the skew angle, which adds shading to enhance the 3D effect
